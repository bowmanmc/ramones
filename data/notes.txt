Recording
----------
- Recorded in January, 1976 (wikipedia)
- Spent $6,400 of own money (amount wikipedia, own money rolling stone/Johnny's autobiography)
- Instruments 3 days, vocals 4 days (mixing/mastering?) (wikipedia)
- Plaza Sound Studio - Eighth Floor of Radio City Hall (wikipedia)
- Mickey Leigh (Joey's brother) and Craig Leon uncredited - backup vocals and percussion


Album Cover
-----------
- Sire spent $2,000 on a photo shoot to recreate the Meet the Beatles album cover
  but was scrapped. (rolling stone)
- Taken by Roberta Bayley of Punk Magazine (wikipedia)
- Photo originally appeared in Punk, but was purchased by Sire for $125 (rolling stone)
- Brick wall of Albert's Garden (location on wikipedia)
- Johnny middle finger - tried to sneak it in but didn't think it'd get used (rolling stone)
- Dee Dee stepped in dog shit after photo (rolling stone)


History
--------
- 38 years (April 30th 2014) to go gold (500,000 copies sold) (rolling stone)
- Sold 6,000 copies in the first year (rolling stone)
- 58 on Rolling Stone's 1991 list of 100 Greatest Album Covers (wikipedia)
- Album Length - 14 songs, 29:04
- 1st single - Blitzkrieg Bop b/w Havana Affair
- 2nd single - I Wanna Be Your Boyfriend b/w California Sun and I Don't Wanna Walk Around with You
- Released 04/23/1976
